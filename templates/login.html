Welcome Weather Observer!

Thank you for your interest in contributing to our weather observation network. Your participation is crucial in helping us gather accurate and consistent weather data.

Why Weather Observations Matter

Weather observations play an important role in understanding local weather patterns, forecasting, and ensuring the safety of communities. By providing your observations, you contribute to a better understanding of the weather in your area, which ultimately helps us make more informed decisions and improve forecasts.

Online Course Requirement

    Before you can start providing weather data, we require all observers to undergo an online course. This course will cover important topics such as:
  • Understanding weather instruments and measurements.
  • Proper observation techniques and protocols.
  • Safety precautions and best practices for weather observation.
  • How to report your observations accurately and efficiently.

Ensuring Accuracy and Consistency

Accuracy and consistency are essential for reliable weather data. By completing the online course, you'll learn the skills necessary to make precise observations and ensure consistency in your reports. This ensures that our data remains reliable and useful for various applications, including forecasting, research, and emergency preparedness.
